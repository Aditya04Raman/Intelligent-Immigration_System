Warning Signs

Common Red Flags to Watch For

Recognizing these patterns protects you from exploitation and fraud.

🚩 "Guaranteed Visa" Promises

No legitimate agent can guarantee visa approval. Government authorities make all decisions. Any guarantee is a scam.

🚩 Upfront Cash Payments

Legitimate services provide receipts and contracts. Requests for large cash payments with no documentation are red flags.

🚩 Requests for Passport Originals

Never hand over your original passport to a third party. Only immigration officials and banks may request this under specific circumstances.

🚩 Urgent Pressure Tactics

"You must act NOW or lose your visa" is a pressure tactic. Legitimate immigration processes have standard timelines.

🚩 Work Without Contracts

Always insist on a written employment contract. Working without one leaves you with no legal protection.

🚩 Unregistered Accommodation

Always sign a tenancy agreement. Unregistered rentals leave you vulnerable to eviction without notice and loss of deposit.

Before You Move

Emergency Preparation Checklist

Complete these steps before arriving in your destination country.

✓
Save police, ambulance, and fire numbers in your phone and on a paper card.
✓
Register with your embassy or consulate emergency service where available.
✓
Keep digital and printed copies of passport, visa, insurance, and key IDs.
!
Buy health insurance that includes emergency hospital care and evacuation.
!
Set one trusted emergency contact and share your new address and route details.
○
Download offline maps, translation packs, and local transit routes before travel.
○
Carry a local-language medical info card (allergies, medications, blood group).
